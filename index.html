about me.

I love to ideate, design and build. Driven by making iterative changes to real life problems, I enjoy devising impact first solutions to make the world a little better than I found it. I use user-centric thinking during the idea and design stages, but move to a product-first mindset when building.

I'm a founding product engineer at a Harvard/MIT-led early stage social impact startup.

Tech stack: Flutter, Flask, Fast API, Postgres, React, Node.

Graduated from IIT Kharagpur with a bachelors and masters in physics. My academic work lies in the overlap between astrophysics and machine learning. My masters thesis was supervised by Prof Garcia at Caltech's Space Radiation Lab, where we looked at light spectra from a black hole to understand its geometry.

Volunteering & science outreach are important to me - I have worked with differently-abled kids, taught project-based physics to underpriviliged middle school students, ran a local library and organised astronomy open days.
